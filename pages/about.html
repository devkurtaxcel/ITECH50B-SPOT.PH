About spot.ph

spot.ph brings routes, fares, drivers, and fuel prices into one place.

Why spot.ph exists

Commuters often rely on scattered updates. spot.ph keeps the basics in one place so trips are easier to plan.

What the platform delivers

  • Live route and vehicle availability summaries
  • Fare guidance for common trips
  • Verified driver details
  • Route maps for quick planning

Who it supports

  • Daily commuters
  • Terminal staff
  • Schools and offices
  • Local partners

Service direction

The goal is simple: clear transport info that is easy to use and easy to maintain.

Government-ready potential

As the project grows, it can support cleaner records, better updates, and more consistent public information.
